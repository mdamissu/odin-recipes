Spaghetti
Description

A classic Italian pasta dish with rich tomato sauce, ground beef, and herbs. Perfect for a cozy dinner with family or friends.

Ingredients
  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)
    • Steps
  • Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  • Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the onion and garlic until soft and fragrant.
  • Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  • Stir in the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, and oregano. Simmer for about 15 minutes.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Serve the sauce over the spaghetti. Garnish with fresh basil and Parmesan cheese.
    • Chef’s Tip

    Add a splash of red wine to the sauce while simmering for a deeper, richer flavor.
