Spaghetti

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish with rich tomato sauce, ground beef, and herbs. Perfect for a cozy dinner with family or friends.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the onion and garlic until soft and fragrant.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, and oregano. Simmer for about 15 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti. Garnish with fresh basil and Parmesan cheese.

Chef's Tip

Add a splash of red wine to the sauce while simmering for a deeper, richer flavor.

Lasagna

Description

Layered pasta dish with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. A classic comfort food that's perfect for family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 500g ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 200g mozzarella, shredded
  • 100g Parmesan, grated

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  3. Brown beef with onion and garlic. Add tomatoes and tomato paste.
  4. Mix ricotta with egg and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture in baking dish.
  6. Top with mozzarella and bake for 25-30 minutes.
